Plant Pilot Guide

Plant Pilot gives you a couple of key insights into the general health of your plant. One of which is the soil moisture of your plant. You are able to track and compare the current soil moisture of your plant with the suggested soil moisture conentration. You are able to access the soil moisture of your plant from any device on the same network as Plant Pilot.


The same functionality applies to the plant's current temperature. You may access the current temperature of your plant and change your plant's environment accordingly. There is a basic recipe to keep your plants healthy using Plant Pilot.


1. Place the Plant Pilot sensor into one of your house plants.

2. Add that plant to your garden via the home page or the custom plant creation feature on the garden page.

3. Use Plant Pilot to keep track of and compare your Plant's soil moisture and temperature with the recommended ranges.

4. Consider watering your plant more or less frequently based on the suggested range. Also consider moving your plant to or from the sunlight based on the temperature range.

5. Watch your plant grow to maturity and live a healthy life!
